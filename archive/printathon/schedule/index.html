Schedule


Saturday, March 7th

9am - Farber Library doors open, teams sign in and set up gear at assigned spots. Coffee and cookies will be available, make yourself comfortable.

10:30am - Opening ceremony, theme will be revealed, teams start printing!

12pm - Boxed lunches will be provided.

4pm - More coffee will be served and selected speakers will give talks.

7:45pm - Library closes to the public, dinner will be served in adjacent building. Only Printathon participants remain in the library overnight.

Printing continues overnight, coffee and snacks available throughout.



Sunday, March 8th

9am - Library doors reopen to the public, coffee will be served.

10:30am - Printing stops, judges and the public will have one hour to go around.

11am - Boxed lunches will be served.

11:30am - We gather for the ending ceremony, winners will be announced, prizes and speeches given.

1pm - Printathon ends.
